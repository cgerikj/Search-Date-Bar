# Chrome Web Store — detailed descriptions

## English (en)

```
Google has a filter-by-date tool. It's buried under the Tools menu and stops at "past year." This puts it under the search box as a visible bar and adds the ranges it leaves out.

Pick a range and the results reload filtered. For an exact window, it opens Google's own date-range picker rather than a custom one, so it's the calendar you already know.

WHAT IT ADDS
• Every range in one click: past hour, day, week, month, 3 months, 6 months, year, 3 years. The 3-month, 6-month and 3-year options aren't in Google's own menu.
• Calendar presets: this week, this month, this quarter, this year. These snap to the calendar instead of counting back N days.
• A custom range through Google's own date picker.
• The bar also appears on Images, Videos and News.
• An approximate result count at the end of the bar that changes as you tighten the range.
• A Verbatim toggle for exact-word searches.
• Follows Google's light and dark theme, and doesn't shift the page as it loads.
• Localized in 10 languages.
```
